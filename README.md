# 📊 E-Commerce Sales & Customer Analytics Dashboard

## Overview

This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze e-commerce sales performance, customer behavior, product performance, shipping methods, and regional profitability.

The dashboard enables business users to monitor key metrics and make data-driven decisions through interactive visualizations.

---

## Business Problem

Businesses generate massive amounts of transactional data every day. Without proper analysis, it becomes difficult to identify:

* Top-performing products
* Profitable regions
* Customer purchasing patterns
* Sales trends
* Shipping performance

This dashboard solves these challenges by converting raw sales data into meaningful business insights.

## Key Insights

* West region generated the highest profit.
* Office Supplies contributed the highest sales.
* Consumer segment generated the largest revenue share.
* Standard Class was the most preferred shipping method.
* Late Delivery represented the highest delivery status share.
* Top-selling products contributed significantly to total revenue.

---

## Business Recommendations

* Improve logistics to reduce late deliveries.
* Focus marketing efforts on Consumer customers.
* Expand inventory for high-selling Office Supplies.
* Promote top-selling products.
* Invest more in high-profit regions.
